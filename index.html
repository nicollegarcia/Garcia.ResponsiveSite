One Big Running Family

John A. Ferguson Senior High is home to the largest and most succcessful running program in South Florida with almost 100 competeting athletes on the roster. I was very fortunate to be part of this team. It became more than just a hobby; it became a way of life. My teammates became more than just teammates; they became family. In my time as a member of John A. Ferguson's Track and Cross Country program, I grew to truly love the sport. On this website, I've shared some of my favorite memories of that experience. I hope this gives you a glimpse inside the life of a runner, or even inspires you to join your local running club.
