Montecristo Sandwich

Although the Montecristo Sandwich originated in Paris, it is a very popular food offering in New Orleans as it cannot be found just anywhere in the country. The Montecristo is an indulgent ham-and-swiss sandwich: the bread is dipped in egg batter and deep-fried, then served with sweet jam or preserves.
